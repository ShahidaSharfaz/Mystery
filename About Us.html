Our Story

A tale of cloth, shadow, and the men who wear both.

٢٠٠٧ ◈   The Beginning   ◈
◈   Our History   ◈

Born in Riyadh,
Dressed for Eternity

In 2007, Shahida Sharfaz — a Sri Lankan-born visionary who had spent a decade studying master tailoring in Paris and Florence — returned to Saudi Arabia carrying a single conviction: that men deserved garments as complex and commanding as their heritage.

With the support of her childhood friend and business partner Huzaifa Fayaz, she opened a small atelier in the Al Olaya district. The name أُحجِيَّة — "mystery" in Arabic — was chosen deliberately. "A man of substance," Shahida once said, "should always leave something unsaid."

"We did not open a shop. We opened a threshold — between who a man is, and who he was always meant to be."
نمو ◈   2010 — Expansion   ◈
◈   Growth   ◈

From Atelier to Icon

By 2010, word had spread through Riyadh's social circles that أُحجِيَّة was not simply a tailor — it was an experience. Clients would arrive for fittings and leave hours later, having discussed art, philosophy, and identity over Arabic coffee.

The brand expanded its collections to include The White Prince line — inspired by the ceremonial robes of historical Arabian courts — and the brooding obsidian suits that would later be renamed The Black Knight. Each line was a world. Each garment, a portal.

By 2015, أُحجِيَّة had dressed dignitaries, artists, and quiet men of immense power across the Kingdom and beyond.

فلسفة ◈   The Philosophy   ◈
◈   Our Philosophy   ◈

The Mystical Craft

أُحجِيَّة draws inspiration from the intersection of two worlds: the surreal, dreamlike imagery of Eastern poetry and the razor-sharp structural discipline of European haute couture. The result is something entirely unclassifiable.

Our designers treat each garment as a kind of spell — a deliberate arrangement of cloth, weight, colour, and line designed to alter how a man feels, moves, and is perceived. We believe that a truly great suit does not merely fit a body. It shapes a presence.

"There is no mysticism without craft, and no craft worth making that does not carry a little mystery."
◈   What Drives Us   ◈

Vision & Mission

I
◈   Vision   ◈

To Redefine Presence

Our vision is to be the world's most distinctive house of men's couture — one that bridges the ancient aesthetic traditions of the Arab world with the boundless imagination of contemporary fashion. We do not follow trends. We render them irrelevant.

We envision a world where every أُحجِيَّة garment is recognised not by its logo, but by its silence — the quiet authority it confers upon the man who wears it.

II
◈   Mission   ◈

To Craft What Cannot Be Named

Our mission is to create bespoke and ready-to-wear menswear of uncompromising quality, rooted in Saudi heritage and elevated through meticulous craft. Every stitch is intentional. Every garment, a conversation between the maker and the man.

We are committed to sourcing the finest fabrics — Italian wool, Japanese silk, hand-loomed Arabian cotton — and to maintaining ateliers where the art of tailoring is practised as it always has been: slowly, carefully, and with great reverence.

◈   What We Stand For   ◈

Our Values

حرفة Mastery of Craft

Every garment at أُحجِيَّة is assembled by hand in our Riyadh atelier. We refuse to rush what must be slow. Mastery, to us, is not a destination — it is a daily practice of refusal: refusing shortcuts, refusing the ordinary, refusing the good in favour of the exceptional.

هوية Identity & Heritage

We believe that clothing is a form of memory. Our designs are threaded through with the visual language of the Arabian Peninsula — geometry, calligraphy, the architecture of ancient courts — worn forward into the present with confidence and pride.

غموض The Power of Mystery

Our name is our manifesto. In a world that over-explains everything, we believe in restraint — in the allure of the unsaid, the unfinished, the deliberately ambiguous. Our clients do not need to announce themselves. Their clothes do it for them, in a whisper.

أخلاق Ethical Sourcing

Beauty without conscience is empty. We partner exclusively with suppliers who share our commitment to ethical labour practices, environmental stewardship, and sustainable sourcing. Every yard of cloth that enters our atelier has a clean, traceable story.

عميل The Client Above All

Our client is not a customer. He is a collaborator. Each fitting is a dialogue; each alteration, a refinement of a vision held jointly between artisan and wearer. We do not dress men — we work alongside them to discover who they already are.

جمال Uncompromising Beauty

We hold beauty to be a serious, necessary thing — not vanity, but a form of respect: for the craft, for the wearer, and for all those who will encounter him. At أُحجِيَّة, we treat aesthetics as a moral obligation.

Meet the Founders

S

Shahida Sharfaz

Creative Director & Master Tailor

Trained in Paris and Florence for over a decade, Shahida returned to Riyadh in 2007 with a vision: to give Arab men garments worthy of their heritage. She oversees every collection and personally supervises each bespoke commission.

H

Huzaifa Fayaz

Co-Founder & Business Director

A business strategist with a poet's sensibility, Huzaifa shapes the brand's identity and growth with equal care. His belief that commerce and culture are inseparable has guided أُحجِيَّة from a single atelier to an internationally recognised house.
